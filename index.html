About Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela Primary School


After Nelson Mandelaâ€™s release from prison in 1990, he visited a number of areas around the country, including Etwatwa in Daveyton, Benoni. Concerned about living conditions there, and particularly about the children roaming the streets because there was no school in the area, Mr Mandela negotiated with what was then Nedperm Bank and the Independent Development Trust to help raise funds to build a school.
